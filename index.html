My story

Born and raised in Uruguay. In 2010 I bought a one-way ticket to New York and haven't left since. Before my life in NY I worked as a journalist for a news website and as a film producer shooting documentaries, short films and ads. Now I work at the UN translating documents from English to Spanish for the General Assembly and the Security Council. Sounds great, doesn't it? Actually it's pretty boring. Why learning at General Assembly?

I'm interested in new technology, big data and how social media is transforming the way people connect with news and information. I believe that the WDI program at General Assambly will enable me to start executing ideas and making things happen. I can't wait to start learning and working in a collaborative environment.
